    About This App

    This Weather App provides real-time weather information, using API integration to fetch data from OpenWeatherMap. It includes current temperature, humidity, wind speed, and a 5-day weather forecast. Users can search for weather data by city or automatically detect their current location.

    Weather Safety Tips

    • Stay indoors during extreme weather conditions.
    • Keep an emergency kit ready with water, food, and first-aid supplies.
    • Stay updated on weather alerts in your area.
    • During thunderstorms, avoid tall trees and metal objects.

    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

    How does this app work?

    The app uses OpenWeatherMap API to fetch real-time weather data based on the city name or your location.

    How accurate is the data?

    The data comes from reliable weather forecasting services, but small variations may occur based on your location.
